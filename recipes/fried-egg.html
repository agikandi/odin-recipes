Fried Eggs!

I think this is quite a universal dish. It's simple, yet spectacular - like potatoes!

Ingredients

  1. Eggs (as few or as many as you'd like)
  2. Salt
  3. Oil

Steps

  • Put some oil on the pan and get it hot! (not too hot though, you wouldn't want to scrape egg off the pan because it got cooked too fast!)
  • Crack some eggs and empty the contents onto the pan (or, you could beat them in a cup first, but difference is minimal, I think)
  • Let the eggs cook until the sides begin to turn brown.
  • Turn the egg(s) and once again, wait for the sides to turn brown.
  • Serve, and enjoy!
